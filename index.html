About Us

Alfaruk Faith Bridge Awareness Foundation is a community-based organization dedicated to promoting awareness, moral values, faith-based guidance, and positive social development. We work to educate, support, and inspire individuals and communities toward a better and more responsible life.

Our Mission

  • Promote faith-based awareness and moral education
  • Support community development and social responsibility
  • Encourage unity, peace, and positive values
  • Create awareness on important social and religious issues

Our Activities

  • Community awareness programs
  • Religious and moral education sessions
  • Youth guidance and mentorship
  • Public enlightenment campaigns
